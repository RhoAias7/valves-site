||THE VALVES|| are a young up and coming band from the outskirts of Dublin, consisting of Glenn Mckeon (lead vocal & rhythm guitar), Jake Mc Guinness (lead guitar), Aaron Marry (bass) and Robert Brown (drums), all aged between 17 – 19 years.

Since their formation The Valves have played not only in some of Dublin’s most iconic music venues such as Whelan’s Live Bar, The Grand Social and Vicar Street.

They have also supported Irish Rock Legends ASLAN in Vicar St and Naas and also Ryan Sheridan at the popular F.L.A.M.E festival.
